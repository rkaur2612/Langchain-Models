In the hush before the morning wakes,
Where silver mist through branches drapes,
A forest breathes in quiet grace,
Time slows within its shadowed space.

The ancient trees stand tall and wise,
Their roots hold secrets, earthbound ties,
Leaves whisper softly in the breeze,
A language spoken by the trees.

Sunlight spills in golden streams,
Dancing through like fleeting dreams,
Each beam a brushstroke, warm and bright,
Painting life in dappled light.

A hidden path, both wild and free,
Invites the heart to simply be,
Where worries fade, and silence grows,
And peace is something one just knows.

In every rustle, chirp, and call,
The forest holds and heals us all,
A gentle world, untouched, sincere—
Where nature’s voice is crystal clear. 🌲
